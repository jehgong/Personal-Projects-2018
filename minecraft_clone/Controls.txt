WASD 			- Movement

Space 			- Go up
Shift 			- Go down

Hold-CTRL 		- Speed up

Mouse 			- Move around to look

Up/ Down arrow 	- Select item in inventory 

Left Click 		- Break block
Right Click 	- Place block

F3 				- Hide/ Show the FPS/ Inventory/ Crosshair

L 				- Lock/ Unlock looking

Escape 			- Exit Game
